Short Bio

I received a Ph.D. degree in computer science from Xidian University, China, in 2011, before conducting postdoctoral researches twice. Afterwards, I worked as a visiting scholar at Peking University and Tsinghua University from 2019 to 2021. Currently, I am an associate professor at Zhengzhou University, China, and a senior member of China Computer Federation (CCF). My research interests span a diverse set of topics, such as formal methods, AI applications, information security, bioinformatics, DNA computing, block chain & multimedia, and I always love to do research in a hands-on manner.
